Bagan/Temples of Bagan

Bagan, an ancient city with thousands of Buddhist pagodas is located in the Mandalay Region of Myanmar. Bagan is one of the world's greatest archaeological sites. It will surely enchant you with its mystical appearance. The temples rise above the canopy of trees, presenting a picture - perfect scenery to visitors. Apart from the temples, visitors can also enjoy a leisure trip on the Ayeyarwady River and take a cruise from Mandalay to Bagan. You will also be able to indulge in the fascinating views of mountains and other sites along the way.
